## Setting up

NexWrite is a powerful Medium-style blogging platform script built with modern technologies.
It’s fully customizable, fast, and packed with features like subscriptions, comments, categories, rich editor, and more.

Launch your own publishing platform in minutes — no coding skills required.
Ideal for startups, solo founders, or entrepreneurs looking to build a community around content.
