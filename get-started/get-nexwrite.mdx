## What You'll Receive

Upon completing your purchase, you'll gain access to:

1. **NexWrite Software Package**  
   A downloadable ZIP file containing the complete NexWrite source code, ready for installation.

2. **License Key**  
   A unique activation key that you'll use to validate your NexWrite installation on your server.

3. **Access to License Portal**  
   You’ll receive an email containing your license key and login credentials for our license management portal: [https://pay.jooj.us/](https://pay.jooj.us/).  
   There, you can:
   - View and copy your license key anytime  
   - See the domain your license is currently linked to  
   - Contact our support team if you wish to transfer your license to a different domain

These resources empower you to deploy, manage, and customize NexWrite to meet your specific requirements.

<Warning>
  Keep your license key secure and confidential. It's tied to your purchase and is required for installation and updates.
</Warning>
